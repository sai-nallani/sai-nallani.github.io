The Problem

Air traffic control is chronically understaffed, leading to delays, safety violations, and fatal accidents. Training an ATC takes up to 7 years, making workforce scaling nearly impossible.

Our Solution

Amelia augments air traffic controllers rather than replacing them. It aggregates real-time airline and weather data, identifies risks for flights within a 40 nautical mile radius, and automates communication with pilots using standardized FAA phraseology.

Features

  • Real-time risk identification from flight and weather data
  • Automated pilot communication via voice synthesis
  • Insights surfaced using standardized FAA phraseology
  • Built on scraped NTSA accident/incident reports
